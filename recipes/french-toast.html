Fluffy French Toast

A twist on classic breakfast

This recipe uses flour to make the french toast fluffy!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices bread
